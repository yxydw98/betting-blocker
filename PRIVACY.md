# Privacy Policy — Betting Blocker

_Last updated: 2026-06-19_

Betting Blocker is a content blocker. It is designed to **not** collect, store,
or transmit any personal or browsing data.

## What it accesses, and why

- **Page content (all sites):** the extension reads the current page's DOM to
  hide betting/gambling elements and runs network rules to block bookmaker
  domains. This happens entirely **on your device, in your browser**. Page
  content is never sent anywhere.
- **The active tab's address (popup only):** when you open the toolbar popup,
  the extension reads the current tab's hostname so it can show and toggle
  "aggressive mode" for that site. This value is used in memory only and is not
  stored or transmitted.
- **Your settings:** your toggles (enabled, block navigation, aggressive sites,
  language, custom domains/keywords) are saved with Chrome's `storage.sync` API.
  This is handled by your browser/Google account sync — the developer has no
  server and never receives it.

## What it does NOT do

- No analytics, telemetry, tracking, or advertising.
- No remote servers; the extension makes no network requests of its own.
- No collection or sale of personal data.
- No reading of browsing history, cookies, passwords, or form data.

## Data sharing

None. No data is shared with the developer or any third party.
